Neuroplasticity

Research

Discuss how understanding the principles of Neuroplasticity benefits people:

The principles of neuroplasticity help people become confident on their newly developed abilities. They become focused, goal-oriented, and accept any failure that they may encounter.

Discuss how you might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your own benefit:

I believe engaging with the principles of neuroplasticity will help redevelop my brain to improving my new skills in programming.

Different ways to increase your Neuroplasticity
  • Mindfulness: It is a fact that your mental and physical health are linked together. One factor that causes physical problems to your body is stress which can lower your immune system and create other diseases. The best practice to lower your stress is do some mindfulness and meditation. It is prescribed as treatment to managed your stress, depression and even anxiety as well.

  • "Stretching" your brain: If you make the brain work harder, you can create new ways of thinking and build mindset structures. It's one effective way to decrease memory loss and any memory impaired related diseases.

  • Positive Thinking: It is crucial to have positive attitude and deal what life throws at you. It is useful to continue to be patient and be kind, and also treat your body and soul as a temple.

Growth Mindset

Research

Discuss what is growth mindset and why it is relevant:

Growth mindset is a belief that if you struggle in a certain skills, over time with hard work and patience you can improve on it. Having that mindset, motivates you to work and accept any failures.

In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?

Having a growing mindset will motivate me to learn new programming languages and accept the challenges ahead of me. If I have a mindset, "I can do it", it will help me focus and aim my goal to learn and accept growth with the challenges ahead of me.

How will you integrate growth mindset into your learning journey?

If I integrate my growth mindset with my learning journey, it will help shape and embrace challenges. It will also help me in the long run to take risks, experience ups and downs, and motivate to do better. It's a matter of creating a routine, being mindful, and pacing yourself with the lessons. It's best practice to be positive and opened to learn new things everyday.
