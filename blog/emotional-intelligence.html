Emotional Intelligence: EQ & IQ

After spending time researching about EQ and IQ, I realised that they both go hand in hand to live a happy life. It's important to center yourself and create empathy on what you do in life.

Growing up in a Filipino culture, we were expected to do our best and level our intelligence to become successful in life. We had to suck it up when things get tough and hold our emotions in check. It is more of the aspect of living your parent's dream of expectation and not your dream in the long run. It is a problem emotionally because mental stress builds up and makes you unhappy internally. I realised growing up, we weighed in more of IQ over EQ since it guarantees success in life. We never ask ourselves are we happy emotionally? Sometimes we need to step back, evaluate ourselves, and communicate with our own emotions. It is finding the right balance to become happy both intellectually and emotionally.
