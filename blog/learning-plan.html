Topic: Technical Blog  Identity,Values, and Strengths

Long Term Goal

My long term is to become a full-stack developer.

Strengths and Limitations (in relation to learning)

At times of difficulty, I've put my mind at ease and think positive solutions to solve the problem.

Sometimes, being the perfectionist I am, I lose track of time and focus to do other things in the project: It's matter of tricking the mind, and starting new things to focus and have discipline with it.

Human Skills

I would like to develop my time management skills when it comes to finishing a project.

Commitment

So far what works for me is how committed I am to finish one sprint per week. I allow myself to learn and experiment on the project given to me. Sometimes it takes a while to sink the information, so it's best to do one mindfulness before starting the work; at the same time, give myself space to breathe and walk out from the PC for awhile to detox from it.

Commitment Aid

Curiosity is a good skill to come by especially when your developing a website.
It is a skill that has been put to use and ask the questions that has been bothering me ever since.

To put that skill to use, I always research online and seek advice from my fellow peers.

Schedule Plan

I always plan my time to work from Monday to Friday. I always see this program as another work day at the office. It's also good to rest on the weekend and get breaks in between to re-focus on the lesson and priority at hand.
